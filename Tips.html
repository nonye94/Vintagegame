Nitendo Sound repair Tip

Are you unable to hear sound from your Nitendo, it could be that you need a change of speakers.
Follow these steps to get your device up and running again.
You will need tweezers and screwdriver to perform these steps.

Step 1

Remove the battery cover
  • Take out the batteries

    • Step 2

    Unscrew that back panel
  • Remove the back panel with a screwdriver

    • Step 3

    Remove the back panel

    Step 4

    Remove the triggers
  • Remove left and right triggers

    • Step 5

    Remove the on/off switch

    Step 6

    Remove the buttons
  • Remove the plastice buttons from beneath the rubber pad

    • Step 7

    Remove the speakers
  • Using tweezers, gently pull the ribbon from the board

    • Step 8

  • Replace speakers and reassemble device

    • Have your followed our steps but are still unable to hear the sound from your nitendo.
